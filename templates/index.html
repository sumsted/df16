#DF16 - Astro

Track your orders with Salesforce and Alexa.

"Ask Astro, where are my orders?"

Track your orders

Enter the email address you used when creating the order.

Provide the order number sent to you in email.

 

How it works?

Step 1

Add tracking numbers to your orders in Salesforce.

Step 2

Astro identifies your carrier then updates your orders with the latest delivery status.

Step 3

Astro alerts you and your customers once a delivery is confirmed.

Step 4

Use Amazon Alexa to check the status of your order shipments.

"Alexa, ask Astro, where are my orders?"
